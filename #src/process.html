Planning to buy or refinance

Whether you’re saving up for a deposit, house hunting, or looking to refinance your current home loan, we provide tailored home loan solutions that allow you to realise your financial goals… often much sooner than you think.

Our home loan specialists take the time to understand your personal situation and work with you to tailor a home loan solution as well as develop a financial strategy that will allows you to meet these goals and fast track the process.

We can organise pre-approval so you can begin searching for your dream home with confidence, knowing what your budget is.

Our unique position as a non-bank lender means we can offer a wider range of home loan products compared to most lenders, resulting in increased financial flexibility for our clients and occasionally increased borrowing power – we can even create bespoke loans to better suit your needs because of our unique position as a non-bank lender.

Ongoing support & management
of your loan

What sets us apart from banks and other lenders is our ongoing care and maintenance of your loan. Not only do we have an internally based, dedicated customer service team here to help you throughout the entirely of your home loan, we also will review your loan either annually or biannually (whichever you prefer) to ensure it remains competitive on an ongoing basis.

If we do find the loan is not competitive in the future environment, we will contact you directly and offer to assist you find a more competitive home loan.
